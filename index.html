Selection Criteria

Select the factors that are important for your organization:

Organization Size

Smaller organizations typically start with PAYG or CSP for simplicity.
Medium businesses might benefit from MCA or CSP for better cost management.
Enterprises typically benefit from EA or MCA for volume discounts and management.

Spending Pattern

PAYG offers flexibility with no commitments for variable usage.
EA or MCA provide better rates with committed spending.
EA provides significant discounts for high-volume customers.

Management Needs

PAYG offers straightforward management for simple needs.
EA and MCA provide department and cost center structures.
CSP is ideal when working through a Microsoft partner.

Purpose

All subscription types support production, but EA/MCA offer better governance.
Visual Studio subscriptions are ideal for development environments.
Free/Student subscriptions are perfect for learning Azure.

Procurement

PAYG and MCA support credit card payments.
EA, MCA, and CSP support invoice-based payment.
EA is integrated with existing Microsoft volume licensing.

Support Requirements

All subscriptions include basic support.
EA typically includes better support options.
CSP includes partner-managed services and support.
